Classic Homemade Lasagna

A comforting Italian classic with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, melty cheese, and tender pasta. Perfect for family dinners or cozy nights in.

▶ Watch a tutorial

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24 oz) jar marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Pasta
Cheese
Meat Sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella, and sauce.
  6. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
