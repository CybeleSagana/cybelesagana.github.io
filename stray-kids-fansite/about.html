ABOUT STRAY KIDS

Who Are Stray Kids?

Stray Kids is an eight-member K-pop group formed under JYP Entertainment in 2017 through the survival show Stray Kids. Known for their self-produced music, powerful performances, and bold individuality, the group stands out for writing, producing, and arranging most of their own songs — especially through their producer trio 3RACHA (Bang Chan, Changbin, and Han). This fanpage is made by a STAY, for STAYs — explore member profiles, discography, videos, fan art, and all the latest updates from the Stray Kids universe.


Debut:

March 25, 2018 with the album I Am NOT.

Rise to Fame:

  • Known for their intense sound, storytelling, and experimental concepts
  • Achieved global success with albums like GO LIVE, NOEASY, ODDINARY, 5-STAR, and ROCK-STAR
  • Broke records for million-seller albums and Billboard charting
  • Recognized as one of the leading K-pop groups worldwide

Signature Themes:

Self-growth, identity, mental health, freedom, confidence, and the idea that “you’re not alone.”
