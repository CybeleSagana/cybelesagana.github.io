Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are warm and golden with melty chocolate in every bite. Sweet, chewy and comforting, they are the ultimate classic treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of any chocolate chips you prefer

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Cream butter and sugars together in a large bowl until smooth and fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and add it to the mixture along with the salt.
  5. Mix in flour gradually until the dough is well combined.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop dough by rounded tablespoons onto an ungreased baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until edges are golden brown.
  9. Cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
