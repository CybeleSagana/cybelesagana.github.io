Major Project: Stray Kids Fan Site

Description: I created a fan site dedicated to the K-pop group Stray Kids. This website highlights their music, members, history, achievements, and the connection between the group and their fans (STAY). It’s designed as a central fan hub with media, profiles, news, and interactive features. Inspired by Stray Kids’ artistic energy, the site uses bold visuals, organized navigation, and multiple pages including member profiles, discography, music videos, awards, tours, fan art, news updates, and a contact section.
