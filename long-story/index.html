The

Pink Lily

Scroll Down

It all begins with a tiny seed resting gently in the soil, waiting for sunlight and rain to wake it up.

Slowly, the seed sprouts, sending up a thin green stem, reaching toward the sky. Little leaves start to appear along the stem.

A delicate bud forms at the top of the stem. Tiny green sepals hold the petals tightly, preparing for the flower to bloom.

Finally, the pink lily opens its petals wide, revealing its golden stamens and elegant pistil at the center. The flower stands tall and graceful, basking in the sunlight.
